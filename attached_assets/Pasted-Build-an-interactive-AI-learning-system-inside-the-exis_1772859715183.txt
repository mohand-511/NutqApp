Build an interactive AI learning system inside the existing mobile app without changing or breaking the current UI design. The colors, layout, buttons, spacing, and visual identity must remain exactly the same.

Core idea:
When a user enters a learning stage such as "Public Speaking and Presentation", an AI Coach chat appears inside the same page or as a small floating chat window.

AI Chat responsibilities:

* Ask the learner short questions to determine their skill level.
* Allow the user to answer either by voice or by text.
* Analyze the response intelligently.

Level detection system:
The AI asks questions like:

* Do you have experience speaking in front of an audience?
* What makes you nervous during presentations?
* Have you ever presented an idea publicly?

Based on the answers, the system classifies the learner as:
Beginner – Intermediate – Advanced.

Stage progression system:

* The learner cannot unlock the next stage until completing the current one.
* Every stage includes a practical task.
* After completing the task, the AI evaluates the performance.

Example tasks:

* Record a short speech.
* Write an idea for a presentation.
* Deliver a 30-second talk.

Voice analysis system:
If the user responds with voice, the system must:

1. Convert speech to text (Speech-to-Text).
2. Analyze voice clarity.
3. Detect weak or low voice volume.
4. Provide instant improvement suggestions.

If weak voice is detected, the AI should:

* Suggest increasing voice volume.
* Suggest speaking slower.
* Suggest proper posture while speaking.
* Provide a quick corrective exercise.

Example AI feedback:
"It sounds like your voice is a bit low. Try speaking from your diaphragm and increase your volume slightly. Let's try recording again."

The system must include a button:
"Record Again"
